# How-Do-You-Build-Trust-With-Technical-Hiring-Managers-in-a-Domain-You-re-Still-Learning-
I've had to build it from scratch in three different technical domains across my career — semiconductor at Intel, autonomous systems at Woven, and aerospace-adjacent hardware through clients like Kitty Hawk and Texas Instruments at AgreeYa.
1.	The first behavior is doing genuine homework before the intake. I never walk into an intake meeting cold
2.	Difference between a job description and a hiring problem.
3.	Help me understand what separates a great candidate from a good one for this role
4.	The third is being honest about what I don't know and coming back with the answer. Consistency over time builds trust faster than projecting expertise I don't have.
5.	The fourth — and this is something I'd prioritize in the first two weeks at Planet. At Woven I spent my first week shadowing the autonomy team's stand-ups and that investment paid back throughout my entire time there."*
